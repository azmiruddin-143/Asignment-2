Power of High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) for Extreme Fitness

HIIT workouts can burn a significant number of calories in a short amount of time. The high intensity elevates your heart rate, leading to a higher calorie burn during and after your workout, known as the "afterburn effect" or excess post-exercise oxygen consumption (EPOC).HIIT can boost your metabolism for hours after you've finished exercising. This means you'll continue to burn calories even when you're not working out, making it highly effective for fat loss and weight management.
